Howdy! I'm Shay Hart

I am a passionate student with interests in data and programming. I enjoy building projects that combine technical skills with creativity, and I'm always eager to learn new things.

I started my journey with the internet and programming young. At age nine I developed a passion for endlessly editing garish Myspace layouts, eventually growing into a small teenage side business of creating Tumblr themes.

I find that most people view the world of information technology through a structured and clinical lens. However, I find it to be more intrinsically linked to the humanities than one might imagine on first impression. Code is language and art combined into one.
